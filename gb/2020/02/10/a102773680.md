<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>武汉女孩绝望日记：爸爸妈妈走了 我也感染了</h2>
<img src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/asia-1177099_960_720-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>武汉女孩日记：妈妈走了，爸爸去找妈妈了，我也感染了。示意图（pixabay）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2020年02月10日讯】近日，网上流传武汉女孩“小杭”的日记，记录她父母因感染“新冠肺炎”，她眼睁睁著看双亲受尽折磨而过世，但她却无能为力，在绝望中痛彻心扉的小杭，在送走双亲后发现自己也被感染了。</p>
<p>目前，肆虐了一个多月的<a href="https://github.com/liahn236/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/442749.md">武汉肺炎疫情</a>日益严重，而在中共<a href="https://github.com/liahn236/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封城.md">封城</a>、封小区、封楼、封户、<a href="https://github.com/liahn236/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封口.md">封口</a>、封网、封死讯的严密封锁下，一幕幕家破人亡的惨剧，正在中国大地频繁上演。</p>
<p>2月10日，一位豆瓣网友小杭（来自武汉）的多条“豆瓣广播”在网上热传，她一家人的经历令许多人看后落泪，小杭的爸爸妈妈因感染“新冠肺炎”相继去世，小杭自己目前也出现了疑似感染症状。</p>
<p>目前，失去双亲后痛不欲生的小杭，因需要“安排各种后事”，带着满身伤痛和随时会夺命的疾病仍坚强地表示：“我要活下去”。</p>
<p>下面是武汉女孩“小杭”的部分日记截图：</p>
<p><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-15.jpeg"><img class="alignnone wp-image-102773688" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-15-600x813.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="542" /></a></p>
<p><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11.jpeg"><img class="alignnone wp-image-102773689" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/11-600x896.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="597" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_102773690" style="width: 400px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/111.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-102773690" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/111-600x339.jpeg" alt="" width="400" b="226" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月19日，小杭的豆瓣广播上开始出现了关于“武汉肺炎”的记录，从描述上看小杭家离武汉华南海鲜城不到500米。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773691" style="width: 921px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/14de46bab476f11291b31b0d0d99d1eb.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-102773691" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/14de46bab476f11291b31b0d0d99d1eb-600x115.jpg" alt="" width="921" b="176" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月21日，仅两天后，小杭尚在抢购口罩的同时，她的母亲就出现了发烧症状。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773692" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/27b2a5d05ebe53c730c92ccbee249019.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773692" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/27b2a5d05ebe53c730c92ccbee249019-600x166.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="166" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月23日，武汉<a href="https://github.com/liahn236/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/封城.md">封城</a>的当天，小杭发出了那条求救信息，与此同时她母亲的症状变得越发严重。在此期间，小杭也是想尽办法为她的母亲进行治疗。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/87599a9c5abd0a92da628da6d47bf8d6.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773693" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/87599a9c5abd0a92da628da6d47bf8d6-600x106.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="106" /></a></p>
<p><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-29.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102773694" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2-29-600x265.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="265" /></a></p>
<figure id="attachment_102773695" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-15.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773695" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/3-15-600x339.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="339" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月24日，小杭的妈妈被送到医院，小杭家人要抢在24点前看医生，因为“只有看上医生，才能被确诊，才可能被收治入院”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7ba66a5ee5eed826ff5ddfa6fd23c006.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773696" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7ba66a5ee5eed826ff5ddfa6fd23c006-600x263.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="263" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而当天小杭的妈妈就开始尝试办理住院，从小杭的描述看，当地医院的医护力量严重不足，而父亲和自己因为与母亲的近距离接触，存在着被传染的极高可能性。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773697" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5461a3fa72a23938e8eed13e200d2513.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773697" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/5461a3fa72a23938e8eed13e200d2513-600x379.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="379" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月25日，小杭的朋友告诉她，自己的外婆在家“中风”，因没有足够的医疗救助而去世。当天小杭母亲也没有及时消息回复，令小杭和小杭父亲无比担心。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773698" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6e870cbd20480488be60253e3aad69ce.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773698" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/6e870cbd20480488be60253e3aad69ce-600x371.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="371" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月26日，小杭母亲的情况已变得更加复杂，医院原本想将其转移至金银潭医院，结果“一问三不知”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773701" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ef88a46135e8e871b160156c67577409.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773701" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ef88a46135e8e871b160156c67577409-600x569.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="569" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月27日，小杭父亲做了CT检查，已确认感染。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773702" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4c39790bc52908bcb1619bd5e9079706.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773702" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/4c39790bc52908bcb1619bd5e9079706-600x115.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="115" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月28日，小杭的母亲去世，离出现“发烧症状”仅一周的时间。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773703" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c228ec1f22d1b6e51d928bed02514b1e.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773703" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c228ec1f22d1b6e51d928bed02514b1e-600x222.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="222" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月29，还来不及悲痛，小杭又带着父亲四处寻医，希望“保护爸爸到最后”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773704" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/859d1b9ce66f946b27ce2d53446af090.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773704" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/859d1b9ce66f946b27ce2d53446af090-600x323.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="323" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>两日的医院体验，让小杭觉得“医院才是最危险的地方”：太容易交叉感染并且医护力量严重不足，自己太担心“妈妈的悲剧重演”，太后悔带着“最需要休息的父亲四处奔波”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773705" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/709cbb04ab4c94f798171c857b75de8b.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773705" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/709cbb04ab4c94f798171c857b75de8b-600x334.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="334" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>1月31日，距离“知道妈妈生病的第十天”，小杭仍在努力救父亲，明明身处“地狱”，但那两天武汉的天气给人一种“岁月静好盛世太平”的错觉。小杭无比怀念曾经平凡的生活。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773706" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/811b8a12356dd4757246e092e1276aec.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773706" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/811b8a12356dd4757246e092e1276aec-600x483.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="483" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月2日，小杭父亲的情况变差，最终还是住进了医院。父亲在病房里“拚命赶我走”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773707" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/40e5c2badfdb5c46e723ff9c1f5ad90a.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773707" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/40e5c2badfdb5c46e723ff9c1f5ad90a-600x241.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="241" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月3日，小杭写下了一段纪念母亲的文字并写道“只是我要求求你，保佑爸爸。现在，还不能带走他。我每天都在拼了命的救他，并后悔没有如此的保护你。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773708" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ca9ad73c145249f6a0bf15e987e1f19c.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773708" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/ca9ad73c145249f6a0bf15e987e1f19c-600x467.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="467" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月5日，小杭出现了发烧症状，担心自己也受到了感染，与此同时医院传来父亲病危的消息要求她赶去签字。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773709" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c4647e9ec6c63671c29846e26cca60ec.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773709" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/c4647e9ec6c63671c29846e26cca60ec-600x188.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="188" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月6日，小杭陆续也收到了许多来自各地热情的帮助，但这种救助对小杭时间的大量挤占也让她感觉很累更绝望。</p>
<figure id="attachment_102773710" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/477d9c8121ae1b90b7cb9694c6b98c31.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773710" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/477d9c8121ae1b90b7cb9694c6b98c31-600x540.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="540" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（网络图片）</figcaption></figure>
<p>2月7日，在好心人士和同事的帮助下，小杭父亲得到了转院治疗的机会，但父亲的情况已变得很差。小杭已经非常不忍看到呼吸衰弱的父亲所承受的苦痛。</p>
<p><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7c307ba6d1f775ace5c99a8763977e9f.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-102773711" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/7c307ba6d1f775ace5c99a8763977e9f-600x243.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="243" /></a></p>
<p><a href="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/43e8c1c53d5e19ae5cd2f2fcf33af92e.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-102773712" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/43e8c1c53d5e19ae5cd2f2fcf33af92e-600x128.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="128" /></a></p>
